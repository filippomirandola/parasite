This is a visual representation about the movie “Parasite”,
directed by Bong Joon-ho in 2019.
The plot follows the impoverished Kim family as they manipulate their way into the lives of the wealthy Park family,
unraveling dark and suspenseful consequences.

The focus of the viz is the characters' movement through different levels of the house, which symbolically mirrors their social ascent and descent.

Every character has its own icon.
The characters’ paths are indicated by lines
The color of the line indicates the family they belong to.
The dashed line represents the supposed path of the characther.
