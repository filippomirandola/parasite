Welcome in this page. This is a project for the
Computer Graphics course at Politecnico di Milano.
The aim of the project was to give a visual representation about
the movie “Parasite”, directed by Bong Joon-ho in 2019.

The plot of the movie follows the impoverished Kim family
as their components manipulate their way
into the lives of the wealthy Park family,
unraveling dark and suspenseful consequences.

As the director said:

“How the characters move within that space is very important. Their pathways, their movements. So, even from when we were designing the space, our priority was how people would be moving”

So, the focus of our visualization is the characters' movement through different levels of the house, which symbolically mirrors their social ascent and descent.

Every character has its own icon.
The characters’ paths are indicated by lines.
The color of the line indicates the family they belong to.
The dashed line represents the path we imagined for the character
when the movie didn't show us.

Also, to further explain some scenes,
you'll se some icons related to symbols of the movie.
Look out for the stone path!

The visualization will expand horizontally but
don't worry, just keep scrolling as you always do, and enjoy!
