This is a visual representation of the movie “Parasite”, directed by Bong Joon-ho in 2019.
The plot follows the impoverished Kim family as their components manipulate their way into the lives of the wealthy Park family, unraveling dark and suspenseful consequences.

The focus of our visualization is the characters' movement through different levels of the house, which symbolically mirrors their social ascent and descent.

“How the characters move within that space is very important.
Their pathways, their movements. So, even from when we were designing
the space, our priority was how people would be moving”

BONG JOON HO, director
